KARMA research

KARMA is a research unit in the Computer Science department at the Vrije Universiteit of Amsterdam. Lead by Jacopo Urbani, it focuses on scalable Knowledge Acquisition, Reasoning, and Management.

Selected Projects

  • Trident, a column-based triplestore
  • Vlog, a datalog reasoning engine based on Trident
  • Laser (Python) and its port Laser++ (C++), a stream reasoning engine based on the LARS logic framework.
